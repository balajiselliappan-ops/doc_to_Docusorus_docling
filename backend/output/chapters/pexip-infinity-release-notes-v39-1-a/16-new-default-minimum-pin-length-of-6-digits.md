## New default minimum PIN length of 6 digits

The default minimum PIN length for VMRs has changed from 4 digits to 6 digits. This applies to new installations of Pexip Infinity only. There is no impact to existing systems on upgrade. PINs may still be between 4-20 digits long, subject to the new **Minimum PIN length** setting in **Platform > Global Settings > Service Configuration**.
