## Microsoft Skype for Business
|   Ref # | Limitation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
|---------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
|   46066 | When a Skype for Business user shares a window and then partially covers the window by dragging another app over the top, participants connected via Pexip Infinity may see a black area partially covering the shared content, and the content may decrease in size. As a workaround, the Skype for Business user should keep the shared window fully in the foreground, or use the 'Share Desktop' option instead.                                                                                                              |
|   45244 | When a Skype for Business user shares a PowerPoint using the "Present PowerPoint' method, other participants in the call may not receive the presentation. As a workaround, the Skype for Business user can use the 'Share Desktop' or 'Share Program' options.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
|   17210 | RDP presentation content from a Skype for Business meeting may sometimes take several seconds to render on VTC devices that are gatewayed into that meeting. One workaround is to use Video-based Screen Sharing (VbSS) instead of RDP for content sharing. If you must use RDP then you can configure your system to adjust the bandwidth used for RDP presentation which will reduce the delay in rendering the RDP content for the VTC device -contact your Pexip authorized support representative for configuration details. |
|   13201 | When a Skype for Business client is presenting PowerPoint slides in a Skype for Business meeting, sometimes only the first slide is sent to standards-based endpoints that are gatewayed into that meeting.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
|    5100 | If a Conferencing Node being used as a gateway into a SfB meeting is near processor capacity and another endpoint in the SfB meeting starts sending content, a participant may be inadvertently disconnected from the conference. To resolve this, the endpoint can dial back into the conference.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
|    4926 | Participants calling into Skype for Business through the Infinity Gateway may experience inconsistent call rejection messages if a Conferencing Node is placed into maintenance mode.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |

|    4195 | Participants connected via the Infinity Gateway into a SfB meeting may not receive presentation content from SfB participants. This occurs if the SfB user has a screen resolution where the width is an odd number of pixels, such as a resolution of 1437x758. If this occurs, one workaround is for the user to share an application rather than their full desktop. |
