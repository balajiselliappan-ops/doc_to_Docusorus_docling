## Datacard® MXD611™ Card Delivery System  

**Streamlined Data Flow** – Both card personalization and delivery systems use a single set of data and file names, increasing data security and reducing IT interventions.

**On‑Demand Color Printing** – High‑quality, near‑edge printing reduces the need for pre‑printed forms and adds visual impact to mailings.

**Flexible Form Sizes** – Extra real estate is perfect for expanded terms, conditions, and cross‑promotional content.

**Regroup Production to Drive Uptime & Efficiency** – Card carrier sorting provides flexibility to remove inefficiencies from the inline process or reorganize finished carriers for maximum production.


Support Market Trends: Meet the growing vertical card trend without limiting job sizes based on card orientation. Dynamically place cards on carriers horizontally and vertically to meet customer requests.
