### Step to fill in PD form  

#### Step 1: Login  
- Login to: [https://myschoolone.com/tbsm](https://myschoolone.com/tbsm)  
  (Copy and paste the URL into a web browser such as Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Safari, or Edge)  
- Login details for the School Intranet App:  
  - **Username**: M-USN of the student (Ex: M-180N001)  
  - **Password**: Same as the mobile app. If you do not remember the password, please use the **‘Reset Password’** option. A reset link will be sent to the registered email ID.  

#### Step 2: Student Details Section  
- After logging in, click on **Student** (left side menu)  
- Select **Student Details**  
- Fill in the required details under **Parent Information**  
  - Required Information:  
    - Does the student have any major ailment / allergy that the teacher should know about?  
    - Select applicable option under **Mode of Transport**  
    - Enter **Emergency Contact Details** (Name, Address, Phone Number)  
    - Enter **Parents' Residential Address**  
  - Click on **Save** (Unless you click the Save button, the details will not be saved)  

#### Step 3: Parent Information Section  
Click on **Parent Information** section  

- Fill in the following details:  

**Father’s Details:**  
- Organisation / Company  
- Office Address  
- Designation  
- Annual Income  
- Mobile Number  
- E-Mail ID  

**Mother’s Details:**  
- Organisation / Company  
- Office Address  
- Designation  
- Annual Income  
- Mobile Number  
- E-Mail ID  

**Guardian Details:**  
- Reconfirm Registered Correspondent details  
- Mobile Number  
- E-Mail ID  

☑ Tick “I confirm that the above-mentioned details are correct”  

- Click on **Save** (Details will not be saved unless you click Save)  

#### Step 4: Sibling Details  
- Click on **Sibling Details** section  
- Fill in details of Brothers/Sisters (if applicable)  
- Click on **Save** (Details will not be saved unless you click Save)
